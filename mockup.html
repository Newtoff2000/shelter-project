Who Kaiser is
☀️ Friendly 😊 Calm 🤝 Good with kids 🐕 Good with dogs

"Kaiser greets every volunteer the same way — chin on knee, eyes up. He's been doing it since the day he arrived."

Kaiser has been at the shelter for two years. In that time he's become the shelter's most patient resident — never pushy, never loud, always there. The volunteers who walk him on Monday mornings say he already knows the route by heart, and stops at the same spot every time to look at the view. He's good with other dogs and children, and his vet history is clean. He's just waiting for someone to walk him home.

How Kaiser came to us

Kaiser was found near the Mafra market in June 2023, no chip, no collar. He's been at the shelter ever since. He came in calm and he's stayed calm — which tells us he was loved before, and will know how to be loved again.

Health

Vaccinated and up to date. Neutered. No known health conditions. Clean bill of health from the vet check in April 2025.
